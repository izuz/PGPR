OBJECTIVES

  • Protect risk population.
  • Have a technological system with all the necessary capacities to face any type of crisis efficiently.
  • Design a viable business model.
APPROACH

What problem does it solve? Protect populations at risk against any type of crisis efficiently.

What is your solution? A very powerful technological solution with business processes necessary to solve any type of crisis.

What is the differential value of the solution? It is a solution developed in the wake of the Covid19 pandemic in light of the mistakes that have been made and the limitations that managers have been encountering.

What do you need to accelerate your project? Experts in the health business, in advanced technologies, investors and the different stakeholders (Managers, Health and users and family members of Public and Private Health Systems and Health Partners, technology companies, Health Insurance, manufacturers of medical equipment, ..)

How could you scale it to another context? The European hackathon offers us a unique opportunity to design a solution with a European scope. Despite the fact that in each country there are differences in population as well as in health and socio-health systems, all of them need to have technological tools that allow them to face crises at the national or supranational level.

What are we going to get out of the Hackathon? We will learn to learn, face a real situation with strict deadlines, teamwork, we learn from others.




TECHNOLOGICAL SOLUTION

The proposed technological solution has all the necessary elements that a typical information analysis platform from multiple sources and formats and with great capacity (Big Data) may need. Since the system aims to efficiently protect the most vulnerable population, it will be necessary to take into account data intake and real-time processing. We separate the elements in the different phases:

  • Intake of Information.
  • Data governance.
  • Storage and processing.
  • BPM system.
  • Indexers.
  • Analytical Capabilities.
  • Graph databases.
  • Dashboards.
  • Dashboards in real time.
